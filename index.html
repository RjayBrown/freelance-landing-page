About

7 Steps,

One Solution.

Delivering Results

Delivering impactful results starts with identifying and addressing the key challenges in your day-to-day work. Whether creating something from the ground up or breathing new life into what you already have, understanding your specific needs allows us to build your ideal solution and create meaningful long-term value.

Development Cycle

1-Discover

Step 1

+

Consultation

We start with a friendly, down-to-earth conversation about your business, the people you serve, and your long term goals. We also take note of any problem areas, things like time-consuming tasks, outdated tools, and customer pain points. This is used to create a solution that truly makes life easier for you and your team.

2-Scope

Step 2

+

Project Requirements

Next, we define exactly what your solution needs. No tech talk, just clear planning and a focus on prioritizing features that add meaningful value. We'll create a plan that aligns with your budget, timeline, and goals to define the project's scope and ensure the development process is smooth and efficient.

3-Plan

Step 3

+

UI + UX Design

We map out a simple, easy-to-navigate layout that puts your community first. Every design choice is made to help your visitors quickly find what they need and feel connected to your brand.

Architecture

We lay the groundwork for a stable, scalable site—whether that means a preconfigured setup for quicker launch, or a custom-built solution tailored to your unique goals. Each option offers long-term reliability with the flexibility to grow. That means fewer tech headaches and more time focused on your business or mission.

4-Build

Step 4

+

Front-End

Interactive features like animations, dynamic content, and intuitive navigation help your visitors engage more deeply and move through your site with ease. It's a professional online presence that builds trust with your customers or community members.

Back-End

We take care of the behind-the-scenes work so your site is secure, reliable, and easy to update. You can choose a user-friendly content management system (CMS) for hands-on control, or opt for a custom back-end tailored to your specific workflows and functionality. Either way, your site is built to grow with your needs and stay simple to manage.

5-Test

Step 5

+

Integration

We connect your website or application to the tools that matter most to your business or community. Whether it's integrating appointment booking systems, donation forms, CRM software, payment processors, or even your existing inventory systems, we connect your solution with the tools you rely on. This helps you stay organized and engaged without adding extra work.

Pre-Launch Testing

Testing isn't just a final step, it's built into every stage of development to catch issues early and keep things running smoothly. Before launch, we run full checks across devices and browsers to ensure everything works perfectly for your visitors.

6-Launch

Step 6

+

Hosting & Deployment

We handle the entire launch process—from setting up your domain and hosting to connecting email accounts and even integrating your site with Google Business. With all the technical details taken care of, you can confidently share your new site knowing everything is working seamlessly behind the scenes.

7-Maintain

Step 7

+

Post-Launch Support

We can handle ongoing updates and security with dedicated maintenance, or you can take the reins with a site built for easy self-management. Either way, your site stays secure, up-to-date, and running smoothly, so you can focus on your business or community.

Our Philosophy

We believe every step in the development lifecycle is important, and ensures the project is carefully planned and executed in alignment with your requirements. Our goal is to provide great service, building your ideal solution while maintaining clear communication during development, so you have real-time information about our progress, and the opportunity to give feedback as work is being completed.

Building the Website

Wireframe & Design

We create look-and-feel designs, and flexible layouts that adapt to the capabilities of many devices and screen sizes. We create structure iteratively using design tools to build sample concepts from your requirements, and we will use HTML and CSS for visuals to indicate a creative direction (color, texture and typography). We refer to this as 'atmosphere'.

You'll have many opportunities to review our work. We'll have scheduled meetings throughout the project (via Zoom or Google Meet) and snapshots of the development site, where you will be able to view the different build stages and provide feedback.

If, at any stage, you are not happy with the direction our work is taking and you change your mind about what you want to be delivered, you'll notify us in writing and we will immediately stop work at that point and terminate this agreement.

Text Content

Unless agreed separately, we're not responsible for inputting text or images into your content management system or creating every page on your website. We do provide professional copywriting and editing services, so if you'd like us to create new content or input content for you, we can provide a separate estimate for that.

Graphics and Photographs

You should supply graphic files in an editable, vector digital format (SVG or EPS). You should supply photographs in a high resolution digital format. If you choose to buy stock photographs, we can suggest stock libraries. If you'd like us to search for photographs for you, we can provide a separate estimate for that.

HTML, CSS and JavaScript

We deliver web page types developed from HTML markup for structure, CSS stylesheets for styling and client-side and server-side JavaScript for user interactions and the website's functionality.

Browser Testing

Browser testing no longer means attempting to make a website look the same in browsers of different capabilities or on devices with different size screens. It does mean ensuring that a person's experience of a design should be appropriate to the capabilities of a browser or device.

We test our work in current versions of major desktop browsers including those made by Apple (Safari), Google (Chrome), Microsoft (Edge), Mozilla Firefox and Opera. We'll also test to ensure that people who use Microsoft Internet Explorer 11 for Windows get an appropriate experience. We won't test in other older browsers unless we agreed separately. If you need an enhanced design for an older browser, we can provide a separate estimate for that.

Mobile Browser Testing

Testing using popular smaller screen devices is essential in ensuring that a person's experience of a design is appropriate to the capabilities of the device they're using. We test our designs in:

  • iOS 9: Safari, Google Chrome
  • Android: Google Chrome on Android Emulator

We won't test in Blackberry, Opera Mini/Mobile, specific Android devices, Windows or other mobile browsers unless we agreed separately. If you need us to test using these, we can provide a separate estimate for that.

Technical Support

We're not a website hosting company so we don't offer support for website hosting, email or other services relating to hosting. You may already have professional hosting and you might even manage that hosting in-house; if you do, great. If you don't, we can set up an account for you at one of our preferred hosting providers. We can set up your site on a server, plus any statistics software such as Google Analytics and we can provide a separate estimate for that. Then, the updates to, and management of that server will be up to you.

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

We don't guarantee improvements to your website's search engine ranking, but the web pages that we develop are easily accessible to search engines with the use of HTML semantic elements. If you need advanced SEO and accessibility features (using WAI-ARIA specification & microdata), we can provide a separate estimate for that.

Changes and Revisions

We don't want to limit your ability to change your mind. The price at the beginning of this agreement is based on the scope of work to accomplish everything you've told us you want to achieve, and timelines provided in the proposal. We're happy to be flexible, and if you want to change your mind or add anything new, that won't be a problem as we'll provide a separate estimate for that.
